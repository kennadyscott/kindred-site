When might more support help?

It may be worth talking with a qualified professional when thoughts or worry:

  • Feel difficult to control
  • Keep returning
  • Cause significant distress
  • Interfere with sleep
  • Affect work, school, relationships, or daily life
  • Lead you to avoid important parts of your life
