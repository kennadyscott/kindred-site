About Kindred

Therapy starts with the right connection.

Finding a therapist should feel less like searching a database and more like recognizing a person you can actually talk to.

Decades of research agree on something the directories ignore: the strongest predictor of whether therapy works isn't the credential or the modality — it's the relationship between you and your therapist. So we built Kindred around fit. A place to understand what you're feeling without diagnosing yourself, tools that help in the moment, and matching that reads the person behind the profile — in both directions.

Credentials still matter. Connection becomes searchable.

Our values

Kindred should feel emotionally intelligent, not clinical; modern, not flippant; trustworthy, not cold.

Warm

Gentle, open, human. Never saccharine.

Discerning

Curated and confident. Not endless swiping.

Grounded

Calm, clear, practical. No wellness fog.

The filter we hold every decision to: would this make someone feel less alone before they ever book?

Two promises follow from that. Matching is by fit alone — no one can pay for placement, ever. And what you share with Kindred stays yours — we count clicks, not people.

Careers

Kindred is early — a small team building the discovery layer for therapy. As we grow we'll need people who write like humans, design like they mean it, and take clinical accuracy personally. If that's you, introduce yourself: hello@kindred.care.

Press

Kindred is a compatibility platform for therapy — a Hinge-style discovery layer that matches clients and therapists by style, communication, and what helps people feel understood. Clients use Kindred free; therapists join with a flat membership and are matched by fit alone, never by fee.
